Noodle Soup
Ingredients:
1 c cooked noodle
1 eggs
1/2 c ground chicken
1 c spinach
1 tbs sauce
2 c chicken broth

Directions:
Heat 2 cup chicken broth in a pot wait until boiling
Add ground chicken and sauce cook until boiling
Add cooked noodle, spinach and egg on the top cover the lid and turn the heat off
