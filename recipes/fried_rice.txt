Fried Rice
Ingredients:
1 c cooked rice
2 eggs
1 c ground chicken
1 tbs chopped carrot
1 tbs pea
1 tbs oil
1 tbs sauce
1 ts chopped garlic

Directions:
Heat a tbs of oil in a pan
Add garlic cook for 30 second
Add chicken cook until half way done
Add eggs cook until almost done
Add cooked rice mixing them all together well
Add carrot, pea and sauce and mixing well
